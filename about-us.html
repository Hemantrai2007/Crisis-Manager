CRISIS MANAGER

ABOUT US

The Intelligent Crisis Management System (ICMS) is designed to efficiently manage resources during crises such as pandemics or natural disasters by utilizing real-time data and advanced algorithms. It integrates information from hospitals, supply chains, and government agencies, using IoT devices to monitor hospital capacities, equipment availability, and logistical resources. The system employs AI-driven algorithms to optimize resource allocation, ensuring critical items like hospital beds, ventilators, and vaccines are distributed based on urgency and priority. It tracks the availability and location of medical personnel, dynamically assigning them to areas of greatest need. For logistics, the ICMS uses route optimization to streamline the transportation of supplies and can incorporate drones or autonomous systems for delivery to remote or inaccessible regions. Additionally, it predicts crisis impacts through machine learning, enabling early warnings and proactive planning. Administrators access an intuitive dashboard featuring real-time visualizations, alerts, and actionable insights to support decision-making and ensure rapid response.
